# Nucleus Brief

Build an animal eukaryotic nucleus as the hero Blender Python-generated
production unit for later Roblox import.

The nucleus is now built by part-level agents, not one giant builder prompt.
See `NUCLEUS_PIPELINE.md`.

## Current Focus

Envelope-system quality before interior complexity:

- double nuclear envelope
- perinuclear lumen
- nuclear pore complexes
- pore gate cores
- nuclear lamina

Do not add chromatin, nucleolus, transport cargo, rough ER connection, mitosis
fragments, or hotspots until the envelope system passes review.

## Existing Outputs

Phase 1 and Phase 2 exploratory whole-nucleus scenes exist. Treat them as
reference and starting material, not as the final quality target.
