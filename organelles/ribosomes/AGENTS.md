# Ribosomes Agent Contract

Build ribosomes as organic, molecular-scale translation machines, including
free ribosomes and rough-ER-bound ribosomes. They must not look like plain
spheres or decorative beads.

Required biological ideas:
- large and small ribosomal subunits
- mRNA threading through the translation channel
- growing peptide exit cue
- free and membrane-bound placements
- inspectable hotspots for translation teaching

Every visible biological mesh must be generated organic geometry with function
metadata, primitive rejection metadata, asymmetry, rounded surfaces, and
camera-readable detail.
