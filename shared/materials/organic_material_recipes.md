# Organic Material Recipes

## Membrane Material

- Base: translucent warm or cool biological color.
- Detail: procedural noise bump, low roughness variation, subtle color mottling.
- Use for: plasma membrane, nuclear envelope, ER, vesicles.

## Protein / Ribosome Material

- Base: slightly matte, saturated accent color.
- Detail: small bump scale, nonuniform color variation.
- Use for: ribosomes, channels, receptors, transport proteins.

## Energy / Signal Material

- Base: emissive accent used sparingly.
- Detail: glow should indicate state, not decorate every component.
- Use for: ATP cues, active transport, stress signals, inspection highlights.
